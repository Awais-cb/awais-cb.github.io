Clipbucket Premium

Project information

  • Category: Web design & Development
  • Company/Client: Integrated Units
  • Project start date: Jan, 2018
  • Project end date: June, 2019
  • Project URL: https://clipbucket.com

  • ClipBucket Premium, a proprietary version of the ClipBucket open-source CMS, was tailored for our premium clientele. I led the integration of HLS and DASH video encoding using FFmpeg and PHP scripting, enabling adaptive bitrate streaming, thus enhancing our project's success and providing advanced streaming services to our clients.
  • I upgraded the video player to support the latest HLS/DASH streaming technologies and developed a revenue-sharing system within the CMS using MongoDB, MySQL, and PHP. Additionally, I provided comprehensive client support, addressing application fixes, deployment, and server maintenance, ensuring uninterrupted service.
  • To boost scalability and performance, I designed a distributed computing system with the ClipBucket multiserver module, with distinct servers for user-facing applications, conversion nodes, and storage/streaming nodes. This architecture optimized efficiency and responsiveness, meeting evolving client demands with agility.
  • These initiatives underscored our commitment to excellence and innovation, exceeding client expectations in video content management and streaming services.

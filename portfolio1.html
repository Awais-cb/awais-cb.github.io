Oladoc.com stands as Pakistan's premier digital healthcare platform, connecting patients with doctors seamlessly through online booking, video consultations, and facilitating lab test and medicine bookings. I started working at oladoc in January 2020 as a Full Stack Developer, and now I'm a Senior Software Engineer. It's been more than four years, and during this time, I've been involved in developing, managing, and improving our product. Every day brings new challenges, and I've been learning a lot along the way. This journey has been both professionally fulfilling and a great opportunity for personal growth.

  • Leadership in Performance Optimization:

    - Recognizing the significance of optimizing website performance, I assumed a leadership role in addressing and rectifying issues identified in the platform.
    - Leading and driving all related activities, I have successfully achieved objectives related to website speed and responsiveness.
    - Ongoing efforts involve further performance enhancements, continuous improvement in core web vitals, boosting search rankings, and fostering business growth by increasing organic traffic while strategically reducing ad costs.

  • Introducing Virtual Healthcare Interaction:

    - Introduced the video consultation feature on oladoc, a critical enhancement enabling virtual healthcare interactions.
    - This feature has empowered patients to consult with doctors conveniently and safely from home, proving particularly beneficial during and after the COVID-19 pandemic.
    - The service played a pivotal role in supporting the community during lockdowns, ensuring uninterrupted healthcare access and contributing to the ongoing success of the company's operations.

  • Key Contributions to Product Development:

    - Played a crucial role in the development of oladoc's two key products, oladoc Labs and oladoc Pharmacy, providing users with a comprehensive end-to-end digital health experience.
    - These initiatives are currently undergoing continuous refinement and enhancement to meet evolving user needs and industry standards.

  • Streamlining Medical Test Bookings:

    - Developed an online test booking system, allowing end users to seamlessly book lab tests through an intuitive add-to-cart flow.
    - This system facilitates users in booking medical tests with home sample collection and receiving reports online, eliminating the need to visit hospitals or labs.

  • Efficient Doctor Prescription System

    - I designed a booking through doctor prescription system, empowering users to directly order medicines or lab tests based on doctor prescriptions. This streamlined process not only improved user experience but also facilitated a more efficient healthcare journey for patients

  • Enhancements in Appointment Booking:

    - Improved the functionality of the doctor appointment booking module to refine the overall booking experience.
    - This enhancement resulted in a notable increase in booking rates, contributing to a more efficient and user-friendly platform.

  • Cost-Effective Search Integration:

    - Integrated a self-hosted typesense search, implementing indexing and search functionalities for collections and documents.
    - This initiative successfully reduced operating costs previously spent on Algolia cloud search.

  • API development:

    - Developed APIs for oladoc's mobile applications, catering to both the patient-oriented "oladoc" app and the practitioner-focused "my practice" app
    - The APIs were meticulously designed to facilitate a smooth and efficient exchange of data, empowering the oladoc app to provide patients with a user-friendly interface for accessing healthcare services. Simultaneously, the my practice app, tailored for healthcare providers, benefited from these APIs by streamlining processes and enhancing communication channels between doctors and patients.

  • Legacy Systems Upgrade:

    - Actively contributed to enhancing legacy systems by leading the upgrade process of outdated modules.
    - This effort ensures that the platform remains technologically advanced and capable of meeting the evolving demands of the healthcare industry.

  • Proficient Project Management:

    - Exhibited proficiency in project management by utilizing Sprint and Kanban boards effectively.
    - This streamlined task tracking and provided clear visualization of workflows, ensuring efficient project execution.

In summary, my contributions to oladoc.com reflect a commitment to excellence in digital healthcare, with a focus on innovation, performance optimization, and user experience enhancement.
